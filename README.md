## About Me

I'm a DevOps engineer with a background in full-stack development - which means I understand what developers need, and I build infrastructure that actually works for them.

My journey started writing **React** and **Next.js** frontends, evolved through the full stack, and has landed me in the world of pipelines, containers, automation, and security operations. I care about systems that are fast, reliable, and built to last.

Currently deepening my knowledge in **SoC** and **security-focused infrastructure** - because good DevOps isn't just about uptime, it's about trust.
